ABOUT CONNECT HOMES

Connect Homes is a prefabricated home manufacturer that’s revolutionizing the housing market. We’re transforming the way homes are built, to make the dream of modern living a reality.

THE FUTURE OF HOMEBUILDING IS HERE.

Connect Homes designs, manufactures, delivers, and installs beautiful single-family prefab homes and other housing solutions across the U.S. Our patented steel-frame modular system makes it possible to build architect-designed homes, semi-custom homes, multi-unit communities, and shelters, faster and more affordably than traditional construction.

We manufacture up to 90% of each home in the factory, where there’s a high level of precision, efficiency, and quality control. In the factory, we avoid issues that often cause delays and unexpected costs in conventional construction, like bad weather, crew availability, and late material shipments. Once we deliver the modules to your build site, construction is completed in days, not weeks or months.
