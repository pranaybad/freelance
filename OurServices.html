DIGITAL ELECTRONIC DETONATOR

Electronic detonator, also known as Digital electronic detonator, digital detonator, or industrial digital electronic detonator, is a type of electric detonator that utilizes an electronic control module to control the detonation process. Digital electronic detonator is a highly intelligent and highly secure industrial detonator. It is a product of the combination of detonation control technology, electronic delay technology, and traditional detonator manufacturing technology. It is characterized by safety and reliability, effective igniting, more flexible and accurate delay, and ease of management.
